You Are Never Alone !

Written By J.R Whitfield | July 16 2022

"Have you ever felt down & dark with no light in sight? thinking to yourself why is this your life? scared to tell others in fear of spite that what you tell them will be used against instead of enlight. so you keep it inside not to swallow your pride although there is help in unfamiliar eyes, someone who can't tell your truths from your lies, outside of your circle theres help that relies. the first step is to admit & swallow your pride so go & ask for help don't bundle it up inside. through smoke & drink, it can temporarily hide but when it goes away you feel the pain that resides. I know the trials of the world can be heavy on one's eyes but try to look around & see the ones by your side, your life does matter your smile brightens lives, and your words have the power to speak positive to your mind, see everyone is different well all one of a kind & what you bring to the world nobody else can provide."
