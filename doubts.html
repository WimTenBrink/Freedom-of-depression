Doubts That Stick !

Written By J.R Whitfield | July 16 2022

"What are your fears? & how is it natural? how do we feel & know how things would be after? is it our mind a superconscious matter? the ability to pick up things through the energy that transpires, like not a word from a loved one & you can feel what their hiding. the unknown & the mysterious are no longer masked. what is spiritual? & why do religions matter? have you seen heaven/hell? & does the afterlife happen? who made up the word faith believing in unforeseen matters? if a lot of things are false how do we know what's factual? if I was to cause a motion how would I know what happened? is it because someone told me or did I see a reaction? diseases that come along are just minded spasms you just keep thinking it until it happens, to see/ not see, to hear /not hear, to live / not live, to lead /not lead. how do we know what's real & not real? the doubt that sets in when the answers do not appear."
